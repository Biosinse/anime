Panda

Biography

Panda is an animated being created under the guidance of Principal Masamichi Yaga, but his nature is complex: he is constructed from multiple cores, which grant him different forms and temperaments. His origin makes him a unique artifact that challenges definitions of personhood. Raised among humans and taught to value companionship, Panda’s personality blends curiosity and blunt honesty.

His different cores can take different shapes in combat, producing variable strengths and tactical flexibility. This literal multiplicity symbolizes the show’s broader interest in what constitutes identity.

Role in the Anime

Panda functions as both comic relief and surprising depth; he is physically formidable but emotionally accessible. He bridges the world of strange sorcery objects and human relationships.

Contribution to Plot

Panda’s transformations solve puzzles and fight problems that require adaptability. He also underscores the show’s ethical questions about creation, autonomy, and the responsibilities of creators to their creatures.
