Kabuto Yakushi

Medical prodigy and mirror antagonist — a man who reshaped his identity by stealing others’ techniques.

Long Character Biography

Kabuto, originally an orphan and spy, becomes a medical-nin and Orochimaru’s right hand. Later he embraces stolen identities and Edo Tensei, becoming a major antagonist. His identity crisis — knowledge without self — forms his central tragedy and eventual partial redemption.

Role & Contribution

Kabuto escalates the war by reviving powerful shinobi and forces confrontations about identity, memory, and redemption.

Special Abilities

  • Medical ninjutsu mastery, Edo Tensei control, assimilation of techniques.
