Itachi Uchiha

A tragic genius: prodigy, double agent, and the hidden protector whose choices reshape the series.

Long Character Biography

Itachi is a prodigious Uchiha who quickly rose to ANBU leadership. Faced with an impending Uchiha rebellion, he agreed to a catastrophic choice: eliminate his clan to prevent civil war and protect the village and his younger brother Sasuke. He framed himself as a villain to ensure Sasuke’s survival and growth. This sacrifice defined his life: exile, concealment, and joining Akatsuki to both monitor threats and bear the weight of his deception privately.

Itachi’s inner life is complex: deep love for his brother, loyalty to the village, and the trauma of carrying collective guilt. He manipulates perception — presenting cruelty while safeguarding peace — making him one of the saga’s most difficult moral figures. His eventual revealed motives force Sasuke and others to reevaluate simplistic narratives about justice and heroism.

Role in the Anime

Itachi drives Sasuke’s revenge arc and later serves as a moral mirror who exposes the corruptible decisions of the state. His strategic genius and mastery of genjutsu influence many key encounters and reveal the cost of “peace by atrocity.”

Contribution to Plot

Itachi’s massacre and subsequent reveal are central plot pivots—his truth reshapes characters’ trajectories and forces political reform in the narrative’s later stages.

Special Abilities

  • Sharingan / Mangekyō Sharingan (Tsukuyomi, Amaterasu, Susanoo).
  • Master-level genjutsu and information warfare.
  • Surgical intelligence & tactical foresight.
