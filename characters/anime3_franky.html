Franky

Biography

Franky grew up amid shipwright culture and rebuilt his body into a machine after an accident; his identity became equal parts engineer and performer. He loves building and dreams of crafting the perfect ship. His flamboyant persona hides a craftsman’s humility.

Role in the Story

Franky is the crew’s architect: he built their ship, keeps it seaworthy, and invents devices that turn desperate plans into functioning reality.

Contribution to Plot

His engineering saves the crew in storms, and his mechanical creativity subverts foes who expect conventional solutions.
